#04.0 Installation

Advanced Hosting Cart – WHMCS Order Form Template is very easy to install and use. Unzip the downloaded AdvancedHostingCart.zip file and you will get various files including this documentation's direct link. There is a directory/folder as orderforms, Go into that orderforms directory/folder and upload all advanced_hosting_cart files from there to your whmcs site's hosting whmcs root directory > templates > orderforms using a FTP/cPanel/SSH/Other program. Go to your WHMCS Admin Area. From the upper top menu, browse Setup > General Settings. Then use the Ordering tab and select the Advanced Hosting Cart as your default order form for all of your products and Save the Changes of your settings. The installation is completed successfully.

#04.1 cPanel

If you know that how cPanel and WHMCS work, then installing RactStudio's Order Form is very easy for you. Follow the instructions below and you will be able to install the Advanced Hosting Cart order form within a few minutes.

Please login to your cPanel Control Panel. Then, browse the File Manager from your cPanel. Go to the WHMCS installation root directory. Then browse templates > orderforms and click Upload button above on your file manager page. Upload the Order Form. Close the upload page. If you had uploaded the Zip file, then unzip it using your cPanel unzip tools. Please make sure that the permissions of your all uploaded files specified as WHMCS file permission.

Please go to your WHMCS Admin Area and browse Setup > General Settings. Choose the Ordering Tab from that page. Select the Order Form you just uploaded from the list of your currently available order forms. Save the Changes of your settings.

#07.1 Sample Product Group

The first task of adding a product in WHMCS is to create a product group. Then, you can assign one or many products in the product group. Product group settings have some useful functions that help to differentiate the groups design and uses.

Products settings will be available after you create the product group first time. After creating the product group you will have to go to the product group setting page again by choosing the EDIT icon from the right side of the Product/Service page. Then, you will discover the below product group options we described here to set up the product group.

Below we describes the settings of product group, therefore you will be able to understand it better and use our product with the product group properly.

Group Name

Specify a Name that should define your whole product group in two to four words and it will be used as the product name in the order form template. Example: Shared Hosting; WordPress Hosting etc... So, choose the Group Name wisely.

Group Headline

Specify a Headline that should encourage your customer to buy the product in a few words (two to eight words) and it will be used as the product headline in the order form template. Example: Select Your Perfect Plan etc... So, choose the Group Headline wisely.

Group Tagline

Specify a Tagline that should increase the confidence of your customer to purchase your product in one sentence (six to fifteen words) and it will be used as the product tagline in the order form template. Example: with our 30 days money back guarantee, you can't go wrong! etc... So, choose the Group Tagline wisely.

Group Features

Group Features allow order form templates to display features that relate to all the products in the group. You can add, remove and re-order features. Newly added feature, will be added to the bottom of the list. All group features will be displayed below the product pricing chart in the order form template. If you don't add any, then nothing will display below the product pricing chart. We are recommending adding a minimum of three (03) or six (06) or nine (09) or twelve (12) group features. Example: 100% Server Uptime Guarantee; 24/7 Expert Support; Recommended by WordPress etc... So, choose the Group Features wisely.

Note: When creating a new product group, you must save it for the first time and then edit it again before you can add group features.

  • To add a feature, enter the display text for it and hit enter or click the Add New button.
  • To re-order features, click and drag the crosshair icon located to the left of the feature label.
  • To delete a feature, click the X icon located to the right of the features.
  • To edit a feature, you will need to delete and re-create it.

Group Template

The order form template to be used for the product group product selection page and the product configuration steps of the order process can be customised on a per product group basis.

To do this, simply select the "Use Specific Template" option in the product group configuration and then a list of available order form templates will be displayed to choose from.

The template you select will be used for displaying of products when the product group is selected from the list of available product categories within the shopping cart, and for the product configuration steps of any products/bundles contained within the group.

Note: If the ?carttpl=xxx URL parameter is used to specify an order form template to be used, then that value will take precendence over the product group setting.

Warning: This choice will only use by the specific product group and that group's product pricing page and product configuration page. Other Product Groups, Review and Checkout' page and all Configuration pages will use the Default Order Form Template from the WHMCS Admin > Setup > General Settings > Ordering Tab > Default Order Form Template settings.

Payment Gateways

All active payment gateways are available by default to all orders and invoices created by WHMCS. You can, however restrict the use of certain gateways on a per product group basis.

Any gateways with a check beside their name will be displayed as possible choices for orders containing products within a given group. To prevent a gateway from being used with products from a product group, uncheck the checkbox next to the name.

Warning: If an order or invoice contains multiple products from multiple different product groups, only payment gateways that the two product groups share in common will be displayed. For this reason it is important to avoid creating a situation where product groups do not share at least one payment gateway in common as that would result in no payment gateways being available for payment.

Hidden Group

This choice gives you the opportunity to hide the product group from the WHMCS site menus on the site front-end.

Note: Hidden product groups are only hidden in the WHMCS site menus. But if anyone knows its direct link, then he/she can directly access the product group and see the product/s.
